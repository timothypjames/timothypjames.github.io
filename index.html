Selected Works.

All fonts look the same.


Featuring work from over 100 type foundries, All fonts look the same was an exhibition of 260 fonts;celebrating uniquely restricted shapes with an almost endless amount of variation.

Exhibition. Posters. Brochure. Curation. Writing. Website.

In collaboration with Trent Kokich, Matilda Stein, Lili Lovell‑Wood & Giordano Zatta.


Allfontslookthesame.com

Bazooka.


A poster series exploring the noisy history of the word Bazooka.

Undertaken as part of the 2018 ISTD breif Mark My Words. An earlier version was a finalist in the 2018 Best Awards and the final work gained me membership to the International Society of Typographic Designers.

Analog — A loose interpretation


An unbound book exploring the value of analog processes in a digital time.

Focusing on 35mm photography, 12" vinyl, notebooks & potato stamped letters.

Diving deeper than the aesthetic surface that each medium provides to uncover the value of analog.
